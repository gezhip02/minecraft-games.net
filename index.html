Features

3D Minecraft World

Explore stunning 3D worlds made of blocks, freely build, destroy, and create your own virtual space. Each block is interactive, allowing for endless possibilities.

No Download Required

Completely browser-based gameplay, no software to download. Simply open the website and start playing, saving time and disk space.

Multiplayer

Play with friends, build and adventure together. Multiplayer mode lets you show off your creations, collaborate on large projects, or host fun competitions.

Survival Challenges

In survival mode, gather resources, craft tools, build shelters, and fend off monsters. The day-night cycle adds extra challenge and strategic depth to the game.

How to Play

1

Open the game

Click the "Start Now" button at the top of the page, or scroll down to the game area. The game will load in a few seconds, no need to download anything.

2

Learn basic controls

Use WASD keys to move the character, spacebar to jump, mouse left click to place blocks, and mouse right click to break blocks. Press E to open the inventory and view available resources.

3

Collect resources

In survival mode, you'll first need to collect wood. Find trees, and use your hand (mouse right click) to mine wood. Once you have enough wood, you can craft a workbench and basic tools.

4

Building and creating

Use the resources you've collected to start building your first shelter. Begin with simple walls and a roof to ensure you have a safe place to stay at night. As the game progresses, you can expand your home and create more complex structures.
