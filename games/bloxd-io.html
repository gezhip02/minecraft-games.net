About Bloxd.io

Bloxd.io is an exciting IO adventure game featuring Minecraft-style graphics and a variety of game modes. Released in February 2021, this browser-based multiplayer game offers parkour challenges, sandbox creativity, and combat experiences all in one place. With its voxel-based graphics and intuitive controls, Bloxd.io provides endless entertainment for players of all ages.

Whether you're racing to complete parkour courses in BloxdHop.io, showing off your artistic skills in DoodleCube, or battling other players in CubeWarfare, there's something for everyone. The game receives regular updates with new features and game modes, ensuring there's always something fresh to explore.

Game Modes:

  • BloxdHop.io - Complete parkour courses within a time limit
  • DoodleCube - Create objects based on themes and vote on others' creations
  • EvilTower - Race to the top of the tower using parkour skills
  • Peaceful - A relaxing mode similar to Minecraft's creative mode
  • CubeWarfare - Battle other players in a third-person shooter with building mechanics
  • Survival - Gather resources and fend off enemies
  • Bedwars - Defend your bed while destroying opponents' beds
  • Sky Wars - Battle on floating islands
  • Hide and Seek - Hide from or find other players
  • Murder Mystery - Find the murderer before they find you
  • And many more modes to discover!

How to Play:

  • Use WASD keys to move around
  • Press Shift or double-tap W to run
  • Use C, Z, \, or Caps Lock to crouch
  • Press T or Enter to chat with other players
  • Press B to open the shop menu
  • Press / to start a command
  • Press O to open the main menu

DoodleCube Controls:

  • Right mouse button to place blocks
  • Left mouse button to destroy placed blocks
  • Number keys or mouse middle button to switch between block types

Useful Commands:

  • /rtv - Vote to skip the current map
  • /players - See who's in your lobby
  • /xp - Check your level and experience points
  • /played - See your total playtime
  • /nobuffs - Play maps without buffs for leaderboard placement

Since its release in February 2021, Bloxd.io has gained popularity for its diverse gameplay options and engaging multiplayer experience. Join thousands of other players in this exciting voxel world where you can compete, create, and explore in numerous ways!
