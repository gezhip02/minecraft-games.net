About Noob Trolls Pro

Noob Trolls Pro is a funny game where you make prank videos. Become internet famous by trolling the unsuspecting Pro with a range of mischievous antics. Be careful not to let Pro catch you!

In this hilarious adventure, you'll sneak around setting up pranks, causing chaos, and hiding in the shadows to avoid detection. With multiple levels featuring different tasks and challenges, you'll have hours of entertaining gameplay as you try to become the ultimate e-famous prankster.

Game Features:

  • Various pranks to play on Pro
  • Cause chaos and hide in the shadows
  • Lots of levels with different tasks
  • Sit back and watch what happens!
  • Released in February 2023
  • Last updated on July 20, 2023

How to Play:

  • PC Controls:
  • WASD / arrow keys = move
  • E = take an item
  • Tab = change camera view
  • Numbers = select item in the inventory to set up a trap
  • Mobile Controls:
  • Drag the screen to walk around
  • Tap on the in-game button to pick up or use an item

Once you've finished your epic trolling and become an e-famous prankster, check out Funny Shooter 2, another popular fun game that you might enjoy. Noob Trolls Pro is developed by Ефим Максимов and is available to play on web browsers for both desktop and mobile devices.
