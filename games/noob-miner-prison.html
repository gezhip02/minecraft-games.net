About Noob Miner: Escape From Prison

Noob Miner: Escape From Prison is a casual game where you play as a prisoner and mine for resources. Get resources, buy new picks, eat cakes, blow up everything with dynamite, and find a way to escape prison. How soon will you be able to escape?

In this Minecraft-inspired adventure, you'll need to explore the prison environment, collect resources, and strategically use them to plan your escape. The prison director will guide you through the basics, but ultimately it's up to you to find one of the two possible ways to break free!

Game Features:

  • Several types of traders who buy and sell resources
  • A large map with hidden resources to discover
  • The ability to upgrade your noob character
  • 2 unique endings (2 escape options)
  • Mine generator that you can pump up to extract more resources
  • Released in September 2022 by Platonov Developer
  • Available on web browsers for desktop and mobile

How to Play:

  • Keyboard Controls:
    • WASD / arrow keys = move
    • Left-click = mine blocks / items
    • Right-click = place blocks / items
    • E = inventory / interact
    • Esc / left-click on the pause button = pause
  • Mobile Controls:
    • Left joystick = Move
    • Right joystick = mine blocks / items
    • Tap on the screen on an empty place = place blocks / items

Learn the Basics

You're trapped in a prison, and you need to escape! The prison director is suspiciously helpful and will walk you through the basics of the game to start. You'll discover the pick shop where you can buy new picks, the food shop, and the director's office, and you'll notice a mysterious elevator at the end that you can access with gold.

Explore the World

Complete the quests given in the top right of your screen. Scavenge the 2D pixelated world for resources and use them to craft new items or sell them for gold. You'll find new and interesting places to explore in this vast world as you progress.

Survive

Like Minecraft and other survival games, you must consume food to survive. If you let the food bar deplete, you die pretty quickly. When you die, you lose all your stuff, so stay fed!

Escape

Ultimately the goal in Noob Miner: Escape From Prison is to break out of jail. You need to work hard to get out, and there are two ways to escape. Will you discover both endings?

Pro Tips

  • Always keep your food bar full to avoid losing your items
  • Explore thoroughly to find hidden resources and secret areas
  • Upgrade your pickaxe early to mine more efficiently
  • Save some resources instead of selling everything for gold
