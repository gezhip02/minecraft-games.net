About Kour.io

Kour.io is a multiplayer IO FPS game that plunges you into intense battles across various maps with many guns and modes to play! It's one of the most accessible first-person shooters, available on both web browsers and mobile. This game offers numerous game modes, including free-for-all, team deathmatch, gun-game, and Hardpoint. Choose from various classes, each with unique weapons, to match your play style and aim to become the #1 Kour!

How to Play Kour.io

In Kour.io, your main goal is to earn KP (Kour Points) by racking up kills and completing missions. The more KP you collect, the more you can unlock! Spend your KP on various customizations like skins, crates, hats, characters, and emotes. You'll level up as you gather points in games, gaining access to even more content and perks.

Want to team up with friends? Create or join clans to form a powerful squad and take on challenges together. The competition is fierce, so aim for the top spots on the daily, monthly, and all-time leaderboards to prove your dominance in the Kour.io universe. Whether you're playing solo or with your clan, every match is a chance to climb the ranks and earn bragging rights.

For a personal touch, dive into the map editor to create your own custom maps and challenge other players in your unique arenas. You can also explore the server finder to discover and join lobbies across Kour.io, offering endless opportunities for adventure and competition. Whether you're customizing your experience or conquering leaderboards, Kour.io offers a dynamic and engaging world to master!

Kour.io Characters

You can choose from nine unlocked players, each equipped with a different weapon:

  • Soldier: AK-47 Gun, 100 HP
  • Hitman: Sniper Rifle, 90 HP
  • Gunner: P90 Gun, 90 HP
  • Heavy: Machine Gun, 150 HP
  • Rocketeer: Rocket Launcher, 90 HP
  • Agent: Silenced MP5, 100 HP
  • Brawler: Shotgun, 120 HP
  • Assassin: FAMAS, 100 HP
  • Investor: Scar Rifle, 90 HP (free unlock with an ad)

Get 10+ kills, and you will be able to unlock even more characters:

  • Juggernaut: Minigun, 100 HP
  • Recon: Vector, 80 HP
  • Pyro: Flamethrower, 100 HP
  • Rayblader: Laser Gun, 110 HP

Maps

  • Havana: Based on the Cuban city, it's the first and most competitive map, set in a tropical paradise.
  • Ghost Town: Inspired by Nuke Town from Call of Duty, this map features an abandoned desert town in Arizona with rooftop warfare.
  • Snowstorm: Set in Iceland, this winter map lets you engage enemies amid freezing ocean views and purple skies.
  • Kour House: The newest map with boost pads, shipment containers, and buildings for strategic fights.
  • Legion HQ: Modeled after the Legion Platforms headquarters, this office space map is perfect for fun trick shots.

Game Controls

  • WASD = move
  • Space = jump
  • Esc = pause menu
  • Left-click = shoot
  • Right-click = aim
  • Shift / C = crouch
  • E = dash
  • B = emote / dance
  • F = inspect weapon
  • 1, 2, 3 = switch weapons
