About Craft World: Building Games

Craft World: Building Games is a new builder simulator where everything is limited only by your imagination! Build various houses using more than 300 unique blocks and decorate the rooms with various decor and furniture. The game features a similar visual style to Minecraft, making it familiar and fun for fans of block-building games.

Originally released for Android in December 2020 and iOS in January 2021, Craft World was adapted for WebGL browsers in December 2022, allowing players to enjoy this creative building experience directly from their web browser without any downloads required.

Game Features:

  • Over 300 unique building blocks to choose from
  • Wide variety of furniture and decorative items
  • Simple and intuitive controls
  • Flight mode for easier building
  • Multiple camera views
  • Ability to hide interface for better screenshots
  • Regularly updated with new content

How to Play:

  • WASD or arrow keys to move around the world
  • Left-click to mine/break blocks
  • Right-click to place blocks
  • Q key to open your inventory
  • E key for action
  • F key to activate flight mode
  • R key to change camera view
  • H key to hide the interface
  • P key to pause the game

Craft World: Building Games provides endless opportunities for creativity, allowing players to design and build everything from simple cottages to elaborate mansions. With its extensive block selection and decoration options, you can create truly unique structures and share your architectural vision with friends. The game was last updated on January 19, 2023, with the developer PEKA regularly adding new features and improvements.

  • Use flight mode (F key) to easily build tall structures
