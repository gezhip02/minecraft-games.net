About Miner Cat 4

Miner Cat 4 is a side-view Minecraft-inspired game featuring a lovable Scratch cat character! Welcome to a world where mining is not just fun but also rewarding. Your mission is simple: mine resources, make lots of money, and whatever you do - don't fall into the lava!

Released in March 2021 by developer Coltroc, this browser-based mining adventure combines the blocky aesthetics of Minecraft with the charm of the Scratch cat in a 2D side-scrolling format that's easy to pick up but challenging to master.

Game Features:

  • Side-view Minecraft-inspired gameplay
  • Resource mining and collection system
  • In-game economy to earn and spend money
  • Character customization options
  • Hazards like lava to avoid
  • NPC interaction with characters like Bob
  • Inventory management system

How to Play:

  • Move: Use A/D or left/right arrow keys
  • Jump: Press W, up arrow key, or spacebar
  • Talk to Bob: Press F
  • Select items: Use numbers 1-6
  • Use items: Click the left mouse button
  • Drop items: Press Q
  • Inventory: Press E
  • Pause game: Press P

Whether you're a fan of mining games, Minecraft-style adventures, or just looking for a fun browser game to pass the time, Miner Cat 4 offers an engaging experience with its simple controls and addictive gameplay. Start mining, avoid that lava, and see how much money you can make in this charming side-scrolling adventure!
