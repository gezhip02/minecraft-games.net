About Paper Minecraft

Paper Minecraft is an adventure game that brings the legendary Minecraft into a 2D world. Choose your character and preferred game mode to jump into the fun! This Scratch game features the classic survival and creative modes from the original Minecraft game.

Whether you're a Minecraft veteran or a newcomer to the blocky universe, Paper Minecraft offers an accessible and enjoyable experience directly in your browser. Mine resources, craft tools, build structures, and survive in a world filled with both possibilities and dangers.

How to Play Paper Minecraft

If you're on this page, the chances are you'll need little introduction to how Minecraft works. The game is a staple of every modern gamer's diet. Creative Mode

An unlimited supply of tools and building materials is available in creative mode. You can fly around the biomes, exploring various terrains like woods, water, rocks, and fields. This game mode is perfect if all you want to do is build cool stuff and share your creations without worrying about surviving.

Survival Mode

Survival mode requires skill and ingenuity to survive. You start with nothing in this mode and must utilize the natural environment to protect yourself from monsters that roam the night. Farm animals and crops for sustenance, mine resources to create weaponry, and build defenses to keep yourself safe.

Controls:

  • AD or left and right arrow keys to move
  • W or up arrow key to jump
  • Left-click to dig or place blocks
  • E to open inventory or chests
  • F to eat and hold an item
  • Space to split an item from the stack in the crafting menu

Platform

Paper Minecraft is a web browser game made using Scratch, an amazing free tool developed at MIT for learning how to develop games. This makes it accessible across multiple devices without any downloads or installations required.

Developer

This game is made by a fan of the popular Mojang-developed Minecraft game. This version is made by Griffpatch; you can check the developer's YouTube channel for more content and updates about the game.

Release Date

Paper Minecraft was first released in May 2013 and has been updated continuously since then to provide players with new features and improvements.
