About Mine Shooter: Save Your World

Mine Shooter: Save Your World is an action-packed FPS game where an army of enemies attacks the world of Minecraft. Step into the shoes of a brave defender armed with various weapons to protect the blocky world from invasion. From machine guns and pistols to shotguns, grenade launchers, and bazookas, you'll have an extensive arsenal at your disposal.

This first-person shooter combines the familiar Minecraft aesthetic with intense combat gameplay. Destroy creepers before they explode, dodge powerful golems, and face off against dangerous endermen. Each enemy presents a unique challenge, requiring different strategies to overcome.

Game Features:

  • Fast-paced FPS action in a Minecraft-inspired world
  • Variety of weapons including machine guns, pistols, shotguns, and explosives
  • Multiple enemy types with different attack patterns
  • Immersive first-person perspective
  • Smooth controls for both desktop and mobile gameplay
  • Regular updates with new content

How to Play:

  • Move mouse to look around and aim
  • WASD keys to move
  • W + Shift to run
  • Space to jump
  • Left-click to shoot
  • Right-click (Hold) for ironsight
  • Mouse wheel to change weapon
  • 1-7 keys for weapon hotkeys
  • R to reload
  • F for knife attack 1
  • Q for knife attack 2
  • G to throw a grenade
  • T to inspect the weapon
  • E to remove/replace weapon

Mine Shooter: Save Your World was developed by GoGoMan and released in May 2021. The game is regularly updated with new features and improvements, with the latest update coming in November 2024. Whether you're a fan of Minecraft or first-person shooters, this game offers an exciting blend of both worlds that you can play directly in your browser!
