About Stickman King

Stickman King is an action-packed arcade adventure where you guide a brave stickman through four distinct biomes. Face relentless enemies and defeat powerful bosses to prove your strength. Each battle tests your skill and agility as you strive to claim the title of King of Stickmen. Do you have what it takes to reign supreme?

How to Play Stickman King

Mastering the basics of Stickman King is straightforward, but perfecting your strategy? That's a whole different ball game.

You start as a stickman armed with a trusty sword, and your ultimate goal is to eliminate every opponent on the board. Enemies are scattered across multiple layers of each level—both horizontally and vertically.

To throw your weapon, hold down to charge your throw, adjust the depth and angle of your aim depending on where your opponent is, and let go to watch your weapon fly. Precision is key—throw too high, and you'll overshoot your target; throw too low, and you'll fall short.

The most interesting part of the gameplay is how you move. Wherever your weapon lands is where you will next be shooting from. This also means you can jump through the air if you keep shooting and have your weapon "land" in the air. Get good at maneuvering from jump to jump; you've already won half the battle. It's the only way you'll be able to tackle the higher layers of a round.

If you choose to play it safe and land on solid ground, beware! You might end up face-to-face with an enemy you just barely missed—so prepare to act fast.

A round is only won once you've eliminated all enemies. Be thorough—your opponents won't always be easy to spot. If you think you've cleared the level, aim higher—some enemies might lurk on the map's farthest reaches.

Rewards

To help you survive the chaos, collect upgrades and rewards along the way. Items like shields can protect you during close encounters, giving you the upper hand when things get dicey.

With careful aiming, strategic movement, and persistence, you'll reign supreme as the Stickman King!
