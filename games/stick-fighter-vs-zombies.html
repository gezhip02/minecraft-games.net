About Stick Fighter vs Zombies

Stick Fighter vs Zombies is an action-packed 2D platformer where you control a red stickman battling endless waves of zombies in a blocky world reminiscent of Minecraft. Get ready for epic fights as you face off against zombies, skeleton archers, withers, and even Noob in this thrilling survival game! This fast-paced combat will keep you on your toes as you protect your stickman friends from the zombie apocalypse.

The game combines the charm of stickman animation with the blocky aesthetic of Minecraft-style games, creating a unique and exciting gaming experience. Fight your way through increasingly challenging levels, using a variety of weapons and tactics to overcome the undead hordes that stand in your way.

Game Features:

  • Exciting Combat: Fight hordes of zombies and powerful enemies in cubic environments
  • Variety of Enemies: Challenge zombies, archers, withers, and more unique foes
  • Epic Battles: Engage in fierce combat, combining skill and strategy to survive each level
  • Fast-paced Action: Quick reflexes and smart tactics are needed to defeat the zombie horde
  • Platformer Mechanics: Jump, run, and navigate through different levels while fighting enemies
  • Minecraft-inspired World: Enjoy blocky graphics and environments similar to Minecraft

How to Play:

  • PC Controls:
    • AD / left and right arrow keys = run
    • W / up arrow key = jump
    • S / down arrow key = drop weapon
    • Space / 0 num pad = shoot
  • Mobile Controls:
    • Use the virtual buttons to play the game on mobile

Take up your weapon and dive into Stick Fighter vs Zombies to experience the ultimate stickman showdown against the undead! Master the art of combat, defeat increasingly challenging enemies, and become the ultimate zombie slayer in this exciting platformer.
