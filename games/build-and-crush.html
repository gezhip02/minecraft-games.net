About Build and Crush

Build and Crush is a multiplayer game that offers the best of both worlds: building amazing structures and then having the satisfaction of destroying them! In this action-packed game, you can choose between two exciting modes - Build mode for creating and Crush mode for demolition.

Released in November 2019 by developer Ctrl4ltDel, this game gives you complete freedom to build whatever you can imagine using a variety of blocks and then watch it all come crashing down with an impressive arsenal of destructive weapons. Make your creations public to let others enjoy (or destroy) your masterpieces!

Game Features:

  • Two distinct gameplay modes: Build and Crush
  • Multiple block types including bricks, granite, glass, wood, and leaves
  • Block variations: columns, stairs, and triangles
  • Various destructive weapons like TNT, cannons, and atomic bombs
  • Ability to save your creations and make them public
  • Real-time multiplayer mode (coming soon)
  • Exciting special events like zombie defense

How to Play:

Build Mode Controls:

  • W, A, S, D: Move around
  • Shift/Space: Fly up/down
  • Left-click: Destroy blocks
  • Right-click: Place blocks
  • Additional controls are displayed in-game

Crush Mode Controls:

  • Left-click: Select weapons from the bottom part of the screen
  • F: Enter free flight mode
  • C: Load another random creation
  • Additional controls depend on selected weapon and are displayed on screen

Whether you're an architect at heart or prefer the thrill of destruction, Build and Crush offers endless entertainment for players who enjoy creative freedom combined with explosive action. Build masterpieces, share them with the community, and then blow everything up with a satisfying array of weapons!
