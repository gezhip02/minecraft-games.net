About Miniblox

Miniblox is an online game featuring voxelated graphics and a diverse selection of minigames. Explore endless possibilities as you delve into a vibrant world of creativity and competition. Join in, and let the voxel adventures begin!

With both survival and creative modes available, Miniblox offers players the flexibility to choose their own playstyle. Whether you want to gather resources and survive in a challenging environment or unleash your creativity with unlimited resources, there's something for everyone in this Minecraft-inspired game.

Game Features:

  • Multiple game modes including survival and creative
  • Various PvP minigames like Skywars, Eggwars, and KitPvP
  • Social features including friends list and custom rooms
  • Resource gathering and crafting system
  • World creation with custom seeds
  • Collect coins and unlock new skins
  • Play with friends or join games with other players

How to Play:

  • WASD keys to move around
  • Space bar to jump
  • Shift to sprint
  • Alt to crouch
  • Left click to mine and attack
  • Right click to place blocks
  • E to open your inventory
  • Double tap space to fly in creative mode
  • Enter to chat
  • P to pause the game

Minigames Available:

Miniblox offers a wide variety of minigames to suit different playstyles:

  • KitPvP - Battle with special kits and abilities
  • Skywars - Fight on floating islands with limited resources
  • Eggwars (doubles and quads) - Protect your egg while destroying others
  • Spleef - Break blocks beneath other players to make them fall
  • Bridge duels - Race to capture the opponent's base
  • One in the quiver - One-shot bow combat game
  • Classic PvP - Traditional combat experience

Whether you're a fan of building, exploring, or competitive gameplay, Miniblox offers an accessible and engaging experience right in your browser. Join the community today and start your voxel adventure!
