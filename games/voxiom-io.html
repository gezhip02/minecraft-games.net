About Voxiom.io

Voxiom.io is a 3D real-time multiplayer first-person voxel shooter featuring destructible maps. The game draws inspiration from popular titles such as Minecraft, Fortnite, Counter-Strike, and Call of Duty, creating a unique blend of building and shooting gameplay. With four exciting game modes—capture the gems, battle royale, survival, and free-for-all—Voxiom.io offers diverse ways to enjoy its voxel-based combat.

What sets Voxiom.io apart is its combination of strategic building mechanics and fast-paced shooter gameplay. Players must build, craft, and shoot strategically to achieve victory in each match. Every game provides a unique experience with randomly generated maps and features, ensuring that no two battles are the same.

Game Features:

  • Four distinct game modes: Battle Royale, Capture the Gems, Survival, and Free-for-all
  • Dynamic inventory system with ammo, backpack, and hotbar slots
  • Advanced crafting system for weapons and blocks
  • Five weapon tiers: Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic, and Legendary
  • Destructible environment with block physics
  • Accuracy and recoil systems for realistic shooting
  • Special ore blocks that drop gems for crafting
  • Randomly generated maps for unique gameplay experiences

How to Play:

  • W - Walk forward
  • S - Walk backward
  • A - Walk left
  • D - Walk right
  • Space bar - Jump
  • X - Open inventory (use mouse to swap items)
  • Hold C - Crouch
  • Hold Shift - Sprint
  • Hold Tab - View map
  • Left Mouse Button - Primary action (shoot, use item, consume item)
  • Right Mouse Button or F - Secondary action (aim, switch block placement mode)

Game Modes:

Battle Royale - A classic fight for survival where the playable area shrinks due to toxic fog, and the last player standing wins.

Capture the Gems - Work strategically to capture the most gems while battling enemy players.

Survival - Craft, build, and survive in a vast open-world map filled with resources and dangers.

Free For All - A classic game mode where every player fights for themselves in fast-paced combat.

Whether you enjoy building defensive structures or engaging in intense firefights, Voxiom.io offers a perfect blend of creativity and combat. Join the battle today and experience this unique voxel shooter right in your browser!
