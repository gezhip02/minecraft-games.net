About MineClicker

MineClicker is a non-endless clicker game set in the Minecraft universe. Click to kill mobs and mine blocks, earn XP and gather stone to buy various upgrades and hire recruits to make idling easier. With 70 levels to conquer, 32 mobs to defeat, and 40 blocks to mine across 14 different locations, this game offers hours of clicking fun!

Originally released in May 2019 for WebGL, the game has been updated since November 2020 to use Unity 2020, making it playable on mobile browsers as well. Developed by Dimofan, MineClicker brings the beloved Minecraft aesthetic to the clicker game genre.

Game Features:

  • 70 levels with 14 challenging boss levels
  • 32 different Minecraft mobs to slay
  • 40 blocks to mine for resources
  • 14 unique locations to explore
  • Progressive weapon upgrades using stone pieces
  • Recruit system to help with automatic mining and fighting

Gameplay:

In MineClicker, you'll slay Minecraft mob characters one by one to earn experience points. You can spend those points on new recruits and on upgrades for your existing personnel. Each hire has mob and block DPS (damage per second) stats that allow you to destroy things faster. These get much higher on recruits later in the game when the difficulty increases.

In the weapon upgrades screen, you can purchase and upgrade your weapons. Just like Minecraft, there is a progression to stronger gear, starting with your hands and wooden implements. You buy weapons using stone pieces which are earned by destroying blocks.

As you progress in MineClicker, the mobs and blocks will level up. You can level up by beating boss levels where the mob boss will have a timer. If you can't kill them in time, their health resets. You can choose which level to stick with until the time is right to move up the ranks. Leveling up means more cash per block destroyed or mob member killed, but as always with clicker games, the cost of new items increases too!

How to Play:

  • Left mouse click to hit mobs and mine blocks
  • Use earned XP to hire recruits that help with automatic clicking
  • Mine blocks to collect stone pieces for weapon upgrades
  • Defeat boss levels to progress to higher levels with better rewards
  • Manage your resources to optimize your progression

Whether you're a fan of Minecraft, idle games, or just looking for a casual clicking experience, MineClicker offers an addictive gameplay loop that will keep you coming back for more!

Pro Tips

  • Focus on upgrading your most efficient recruits first
  • Save resources for boss fights rather than immediately spending them
  • Try to balance between mob killing and block mining upgrades
  • Don't rush to higher levels until you have strong enough weapons
