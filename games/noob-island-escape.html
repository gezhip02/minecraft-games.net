About Noob: Island Escape

Noob: Island Escape is a casual adventure game where you help Noob build a new ship and escape from the island! Collect resources and build the means required to escape the island. How long can you survive?

This fun game takes inspiration from voxelated classics like Minecraft and popular blocky games like Bloxd.io, but adds an element of simplicity for a casual pace of gameplay. Perfect for players who enjoy survival challenges with a blocky aesthetic.

Game Features:

  • Colorful voxel-based island environment to explore
  • Resource collection and crafting mechanics
  • Ship-building gameplay elements
  • Simple, intuitive controls for all ages
  • Casual gameplay with survival challenges
  • Charming blocky graphics style

How to Play:

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character around the island
  • Left mouse button can also be used for movement
  • Collect resources by approaching them
  • Follow the in-game objectives to build your escape ship
  • Manage your resources wisely to survive longer

Released in January 2023 by S_Industries, Noob: Island Escape offers a delightful casual adventure that's easy to pick up and play right in your browser. The game works on both desktop and mobile platforms, making it accessible wherever you want to play. Last updated on January 27, 2023, this game continues to be a favorite among fans of survival and building games.
