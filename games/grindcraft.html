About GrindCraft

GrindCraft is a Minecraft-themed clicker game focused on the crafting aspect of Minecraft. This addictive gameplay strips away the 3D world so you can concentrate solely on collecting resources and climbing the crafting ladder to create stronger items and tools.

Starting with just your fists, you can beat down trees for wood and begin collecting various raw materials just like in the survival mode of Minecraft. As you gather more resources, you'll be able to craft wooden tools like an axe and spade that make collecting additional resources much easier and more efficient.

Game Features:

  • Fast clicker-style Minecraft gameplay
  • Craft various items and tools from the Minecraft universe
  • Four click boxes: overworld, mine, food, and mobs
  • Build a village with various workers to automate resource collection
  • Hover over craftable items to see required resources
  • Sit back and watch your GrindCraft society grow!

How to Play:

  • Use your mouse to click on resources in the four click boxes
  • Collect various raw materials to unlock new crafting options
  • Hover over items to see what resources you need to craft them
  • Build better tools to make collecting resources easier
  • Create villagers to automate your resource collection
  • The more villagers you have, the faster your resources accumulate

The grinding takes place in four click boxes: overworld, mine, food, and mobs. The variety of items you can harvest depends on the gear you currently have - for example, it won't show lava if you don't have a bucket to collect it. GrindCraft shows raw mineable resources at frequencies based on their rarity in the Minecraft world.

Once you have enough resources to build a village, you can create villagers to drastically speed up your production by automating the resource collection process. This allows you to sit back and watch as your GrindCraft society grows and thrives!
